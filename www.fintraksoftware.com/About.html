About us

because one size doesn't fit all.

LEARN MORE

FinTrak Software is a global Financial Technology organization providing innovative technology and business solutions to financial institutions in the financial services sector and enterprises across continents.

FinTrak Software is a global company, with business offices in Nigeria, Ghana and Gambia . Armed with and army of software engineers and professionals with competencies across banking, finance, audit, consulting and software development, FinTrak Software is on a mission to support organizations and states with technologies and intellectual strength required to enable them surpass their stakeholders’ expectations. 

Our trusted clients cut across financial services, logistics, insurance and manufacturing. Our reporting solutions is one of the most reliable in sub Saharan Africa. From management information systems implementation or improvement to intuitive financial reports with self service capabilities that meets both regulatory and internal expectations. 

With Over 90 staff inclusive of 60 Software Engineers based in Nigeria, we have successfully implemented over 100 technology solutions for various enterprises engaged in Commercial Banking, Mortgage Banking, Insurance and Manufacturing across: Nigeria, Benin, Togo, Cote D'Ivoire, Gambia, Sierra Leone, Ghana, Senegal, Rwanda, Congo, Zambia and more in progress from our business offices in Nigeria, Ghana and Gambia.
